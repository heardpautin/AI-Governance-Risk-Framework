# AI Governance & Risk Framework (Portfolio Project)

## Overview
This project demonstrates a practical AI governance approach for a regulated organization. It includes an AI system inventory, an AI risk register, an access review model, an acceptable use policy, and an executive risk posture summary.

## Objectives
- Establish a repeatable AI governance process
- Document ownership and accountability (Business vs Technical Owner)
- Classify data exposure and regulatory impact
- Define access review cadence based on risk tier
- Track risks with controls, mitigations, owners, and target dates
- Provide leadership-ready reporting

## Deliverables
- **AI System Inventory** (Inventory of AI systems with ownership, classification, exposure, and review frequency)
- **AI Risk Register** (Risks tied to each AI system with mitigations and owners)
- **Access Review Model** (Review cadence + ownership model)
- **AI Acceptable Use & Governance Policy** (Policy draft aligned with governance requirements)
- **Executive Risk Posture Summary** (One-page leadership brief)

## How to Use
1. Start with `01_AI-System-Inventory/`
2. Review risks in `02_AI-Risk-Register/`
3. Apply review cadence and ownership from `03_Access-Review-Model/`
4. Use policy documents in `04_Policies/`
5. Reference the executive summary in `05_Executive-Brief/`

## Notes
- All systems and data are representative examples for governance demonstration.
- No proprietary or sensitive organizational information is included.
